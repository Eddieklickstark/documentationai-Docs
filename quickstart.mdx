## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info">
  Before you begin, ensure you have a valid email address and access to your CRM credentials (Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive). The entire setup takes less than 10 minutes.
</Callout>

## Step 1: Create Your Account

Navigate to the [addsales Dashboard](https://dashboard.example.com) and sign up with your work email. You will receive a verification link instantly.

After confirming your email, set a strong password and accept the terms. Your workspace is created automatically.

## Step 2: Connect Your CRM

Use the integration wizard to link your data sources.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Salesforce" icon="cloud">
    Enter your Salesforce instance URL and generate an OAuth token.

````bash
curl -X POST https://api.example.com/integrations/salesforce \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{"instance_url": "https://yourcompany.salesforce.com"}'
````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="HubSpot" icon="database">
    Provide your HubSpot API key and select the pipelines to sync.

````bash
curl -X POST https://api.example.com/integrations/hubspot \
  -H "Authorization: Bearer YOUR_API_KEY" \
  -d '{"api_key": "your-hubspot-key"}'
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Step 3: Run Your First Analysis

Once connected, trigger an initial sales structure analysis.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select Data Range" icon="bar-chart">
    Choose the last 90 days of deals and leads from the dropdown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Parameters" icon="settings">
    Enable AI scoring and set your target close rate to 25%.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Execute Analysis" icon="play-circle">
    Click **Run Analysis**. Results appear in under 60 seconds.

````json
{
  "analysis_id": "ana_9k2mX7pQ",
  "pipeline_health": 78,
  "opportunities": 142,
  "bottleneck_stage": "qualification"
}
````
  </Step>
</Steps>
